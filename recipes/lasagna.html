Pasta a la vodka

Pasta a la vodka is a delicious Italian dish that is made using a creamy tomato paste, with your choice of any kind of pasta (preferrably thicker pastas, like Penne, or tortellini.) topped off, of course, with a healthy serving of shredded mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 Lb Penne
  • 3 cloves of Garlic
  • 1 whole Onion, finely chopped
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup of vodka
  • 1 14 oz can tomato puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 pinch red pepper flakes
  • salt
  • ground black pepper
  • grated Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to directions
  2. Sautee garlic and onions in 2 tbsp butter and the olive oil, medium heat
  3. add vodka to pan w/Garlic
  4. Leave mix w Vodka in pan for 2-3 min, then add tomato puree and stir until well mixed
  5. reduce heat to low, and add heavy cream
  6. Reduce to low simmer, and add red pepper flakes to the mix
  7. Add salt and pepper to taste
  8. Mix in remaining tbsp of butter
  9. Add drained noodles and Voila!
